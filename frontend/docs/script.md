# Maroon - Demo Video Script (3-4 min)

Bilingual script for the video editor. Every beat has:
- **Scene / Visual** - what to put on screen (screen recording, logo, text).
- **VO (EN)** - English narration (this is what the viewer hears).
- **VO (ID)** - Indonesian translation so the editor understands the message.
- **On-screen text** - overlays / lower-thirds to add.
- **Editor note** - pacing, music, transitions, in plain language.

Total target: **3:00 - 4:00**. Tone: clean, modern, consumer-fintech (think a
banking app ad, not a crypto tutorial). Music: upbeat, minimal, builds slowly.

Sponsors to highlight clearly (logos + callouts): **Particle Network** and
**Magic Labs**. Keep their logos on screen during the relevant sections.

Quick term guide for the editor (so the voiceover makes sense):
- **Vault** = a place your money earns interest (like a high-yield savings pot).
- **Yield / APY** = the interest rate per year.
- **Wallet / EOA** = a crypto account.
- **Chain** = a separate network (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, BNB...). Normally
  users must juggle many. Maroon hides that.
- **Gas** = the network fee. In Maroon the user never has to think about it.

then hard cut to the clean Maroon home screen. End the hook on the Maroon logo.

**VO (EN):** "Earning real DeFi yield today means wallets, seed phrases, gas,
and ten different networks. What if it was just... one tap?"

**VO (ID):** "Dapat imbal hasil DeFi sekarang ribet: dompet, seed phrase, gas,
sepuluh jaringan beda. Gimana kalau cukup... satu ketuk?"

**On-screen text:** "DeFi yield. Without the DeFi headache."

**Editor note:** Snappy cuts on the beat. The messy crypto shots should feel
stressful; the Maroon cut should feel like relief (light, calm, breathe).

## 4. HOW IT WORKS (walkthrough)  (1:20 - 2:50)

> Editor: this is the core demo. Record each step as clean screen capture at
> high resolution. Add a small numbered chip in the corner ("Step 1", "Step 2")
> so viewers can follow. Keep the cursor movements slow and deliberate.

### 4a. Log in with Magic  (approx 12s)

**Scene / Visual:** Click "Log In". The Magic email login appears. Type an email
(use a gmail address for the demo), receive the code, and land logged in. Show
the "Secured by Magic" badge.

**VO (EN):** "Sign up with just an email, powered by Magic. No seed phrase, no
extension. Behind the scenes, Magic creates a real self-custodial wallet for
you."

**VO (ID):** "Daftar cukup pakai email, didukung Magic. Tanpa seed phrase, tanpa
extension. Di belakang layar, Magic bikin dompet self-custody asli buat kamu."

**On-screen text:** "Login: email only  -  Powered by Magic Labs"

**Editor note:** Keep the Magic logo visible. This is a sponsor moment - hold
the "Secured by Magic" frame for ~1 second.

### 4b. Browse real vaults  (approx 15s)

**Scene / Visual:** Scroll the vault marketplace. Hover the category tabs
(Trending, DeFi, Best yield, Stablecoins, Liquid staking, Lending, Low risk,
High risk). Click a chain filter (Arbitrum / Base / Ethereum / BNB). Tap the
bookmark icon on a vault to save it, then open the Saved menu.

**VO (EN):** "Browse live vaults from protocols like Aave, Morpho, Pendle and
more - real yields, real TVL, pulled straight from LI.FI. Filter by category or
chain, and bookmark the ones you like."

**VO (ID):** "Jelajahi vault langsung dari protokol seperti Aave, Morpho,
Pendle, dan lainnya - imbal hasil nyata, TVL nyata, ditarik langsung dari LI.FI.
Filter per kategori atau chain, dan simpan yang kamu suka."

**On-screen text:** "Live data by LI.FI  -  Save your favorites"

**Editor note:** Show the numbers (APY, total deposited) clearly - zoom slightly
on a vault card so the yields are readable.

### 4c. Deposit into a vault  (approx 20s)

**Scene / Visual:** Click "Earn" on a vault (e.g. Aave USDC on Arbitrum). The
deposit popup opens. Show the "Pay with" token row (USDC, USDT, ETH, BNB, SOL)
with logos. Pick USDC, type an amount. Point at the "Cross-chain deposit" note.
Point at "Network fee: Free". Click "Confirm deposit". Show the success screen
with the transaction hash and the "LI.FI Scan" and explorer links.

**VO (EN):** "Depositing is one tap. Pay with any token - USDC, ETH, even from
another chain - and Maroon routes it into the vault for you. No gas to buy, no
bridging to figure out. When it's done, you get a real transaction you can
verify on-chain."

**VO (ID):** "Deposit cukup satu ketuk. Bayar pakai token apa aja - USDC, ETH,
bahkan dari chain lain - dan Maroon yang mengarahkannya ke vault. Nggak perlu
beli gas, nggak perlu pusing bridging. Selesai, kamu dapat transaksi asli yang
bisa dicek on-chain."

**On-screen text:** "Pay with any token  -  Cross-chain  -  Gasless for you"

**Editor note:** This is the money shot. Slow down on the "Cross-chain deposit"
line and the "Free" network fee. On the success screen, briefly click the
"LI.FI Scan" link to show it opens a real explorer (proof it's real).

### 4d. Invest with AI  (approx 20s)

**Scene / Visual:** Tap "Invest with AI" in the top bar. Type a goal and an
amount (e.g. "grow my savings safely", $100). Watch "Maroon AI" think, then
show the plan: money split across several vaults with a blended yield. Tap a
nudge chip (Balanced / Make it safer / Chase more yield / Keep it simple) and
show the plan update. Tap Deposit to open the simulation, then Confirm.

**VO (EN):** "Not sure where to put your money? Just tell Maroon AI your goal. It
builds a diversified plan across multiple vaults and chains, shows you the
blended yield, and lets you make it safer or bolder with one tap. Preview the
whole thing, then deposit the entire basket at once."

**VO (ID):** "Bingung mau taruh uang di mana? Cukup kasih tahu tujuanmu ke Maroon
AI. Dia bikin rencana terdiversifikasi di beberapa vault dan chain, nunjukin
imbal hasil gabungan, dan bisa dibikin lebih aman atau lebih agresif cukup satu
ketuk. Lihat simulasinya dulu, terus deposit seluruh keranjang sekaligus."

**On-screen text:** "Maroon AI  -  a diversified plan in seconds"

**Editor note:** Show the plan bar splitting into colored segments (the
allocation). The nudge chips changing the plan is a great visual - capture that
re-think animation.

### 4e. Check your portfolio  (approx 15s)

**Scene / Visual:** Open the profile / portfolio page. Show the Balance,
Deposited and Blended APY stats. Switch between the Positions, Holdings and
Activity tabs. Show a position (Aave USDC, Arbitrum) and an activity entry.

**VO (EN):** "Everything lives in one portfolio. See your balance, what you've
deposited, your blended yield, your positions across every chain, and your
recent activity - all in one place."

**VO (ID):** "Semuanya ada di satu portfolio. Lihat saldo, yang sudah kamu
deposit, imbal hasil gabungan, posisi di semua chain, dan aktivitas terbaru -
semua di satu tempat."

**On-screen text:** "One portfolio  -  every chain"

**Editor note:** Clean, quick tab switches. This closes the loop: deposit ->
see it in the portfolio.

> Editor: this section is for the judges. Keep the sponsor logos big and on
